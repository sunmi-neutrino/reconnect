
### About This Project
Create a high fidelity ReConnect Web AppReConnect will become a central marketing hub, replacing the AMP app. Its purpose expands from a simple Canva data feed to a visible platform for sharing marketing assets.

Unified Platform: The new "ReConnect Base" will consolidate all features (AMP, sharing, showcases) into a single app, eliminating the need for separate product switches.

The platform will support three distinct marketing products:
- AMP: Single-listing marketing (Websites, flyers, QR codes, Social posts, Assets).
- Showcases: Multi-listing marketing (websites, Assets social posts, embed codes).
- Company Marketing: Brokerage-wide assets and campaigns

Freemium Model: A free tier provides read-only access to shared content, acting as a lead-gen funnel to convert users to paid plans for creating their own marketing.

Reconnect user will share their listings/showcases with their network by inviting them. Simple email invitation.
